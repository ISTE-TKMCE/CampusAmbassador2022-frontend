About Athena

Athena is the annual technical fest of ISTE Students Chapter TKMCE. Athena provides a manifesto of opportunities to showcase and enrich young talents and transforms their creativity into reality.

FAQ

Who is a Campus Ambassador?

Campus Ambassador is the face of the fest. They represent and publicize the events on their respective colleges.

Who is eligible to apply for CA?

Any college student with a valid college student ID Card is eligible to apply for the post.

How can you apply for the event ?

You can be one of the CA representing your college by registering from this portal.Click on to the Register button to initiate registration

Is registration fee required?

Of course not ,the event is completely free.

When are you eliminated from the program?

Any participant found involved in malpractices are banned from the event .The decision of the organizing committee will be final.

Is it possible for a college to have more than one CA?

Yes,we can have more than one college ambassador from a college if there are many registrations.
